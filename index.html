About Us

Welcome to Thai Chinpala Kitchen!

Based in Ashgrove, Brisbane, we aim to serve good quality Thai food and create a vibrant family-friendly atmosphere.

Our Chef and Owner, Mr Somchit Chinpala, has years of experience working as a Head Chef in 5-star hotels all around the world, including Jakarta, Istanbul, Malaysia and Hayman Island. Somchit is also featured in the Who's Who of International Chefs

His lifelong dream came true when he opened Thai Chinpala Kitchen in Oct 2010 and his journey as an entrepreneur began. He hopes his chinpala kitchen will become one of your favourite Thai restaurants.

Somchit and his dedicated team do their very best to serve you as a customer. If you have any suggestions, recommendations or complaints about our service, we woud love to hear from you.
